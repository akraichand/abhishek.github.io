  • Research and Development of an Educational Material Search System for the Next-Generation Education Environment, CourseShare - A online search system for learning slides (2011-2014)
    Developing a vertical search system specialized in learning materials and to provide users with an easy access to high quality learning materials in an integrative and intuitive way. CourseShare is a working prototype of search system aiming at significantly improving educational efficiency by developing a search system through which the vast number of online educational materials is properly indexed and utilized. We define four requirements with specific objectives for the development of this system: Searching learning materials in consideration of semantics; Query recommendation and learning materials data mining; Personalized search technique that enhances the utilization of learning materials; Ranking method based on the quality of learning materials.
    (This research is supported by the National Research Foundation of Korea (NRF) funded by the ministry of Education, Science and Technology, Sept. 2011 ~ Aug. 2014.)
  • An Intelligent System of Extracting, Integrating and Searching Semi-Structure Data for Better Multidisciplinary Collaboration and Document Understanding
    Supporting the unprecedented semi-structured data integration across spatial and temporal scales; Facilitating the interdisciplinary research among researchers in different fields; Breaks the carriers between semi-structured data and other data types, such as the free-text documents or the strictly managed databases; Speeding up the learning process with the powerful integration of the experimental, analytical and simulation in the related areas by extracting, integrating, and searching semi-structured data semantically. Proposing a more flexible document summarization and content displaying way for mobile devices.
    (This research is supported by the National Research Foundation of Korea (NRF) funded by the ministry of Education, Science and Technology, May 2011 ~ May 2013.)
